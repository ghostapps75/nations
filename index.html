How to Play

Welcome to Nations! The classic card game of "War" combined with global geography stats.

  • Face Off: You hold one country card face-up. Aidan holds his face-down.
  • Pick Your Stat: On your turn, tap the stat on your card you think can beat Aidan's hidden card!
  • Alternating Turns: You and Aidan will take turns choosing the category to compete on, regardless of who wins the previous round!
  • Challenge Override: If you draw a terrible card, the Challenge button will glow. Tap it to answer a trivia question and steal the round!
