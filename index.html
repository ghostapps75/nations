How to Play

It plays just like the card game "War" or "Top Trumps," but with geography:

  • Face Off: You get a country card. Aidan holds a card face-down.
  • Pick Your Stat: Look at your stats and tap the one you think will beat Aidan.
  • The Reveal: If your number is higher, you win the round.
  • Winning Streak: If you win, you keep your turn!
  • Risk It: Don't like your stats? Tap "CHALLENGE AIDAN"! Aidan will pick a quiz. Get it right to steal his card!
